Special Needs Scholarships & Job Programs

Scholarships

Microsoft Disability Scholarship – Microsoft (D&I Initiative)
For high school seniors with disabilities pursuing tech-related degrees. Offers $5,000/year up to $20,000 total. Apply here.

Google Lime Scholarship – Google & Lime Connect
For undergraduate students with visible or invisible disabilities in tech. $10,000 one-time award. Apply here.

AAHD Frederick J. Krause Scholarship – American Association on Health & Disability
For college students with disabilities majoring in health/disability-related fields. Around $1,000 annually. Apply here.

Anne Ford Scholarship & Allegra Ford Thomas Scholarship – NCLD
For students with learning disabilities or ADHD entering 2-year or 4-year colleges. Awards up to $10,000 and $5,000 respectively.

OAR Autism Scholarships – Organization for Autism Research
For autistic students in college or vocational programs. $3,000 per recipient. Apply here.

NFB National Scholarship – National Federation of the Blind
$8,000 awards for blind/visually impaired students. Includes trip to NFB national convention. Apply here.

Internships

AAPD Summer Internship – American Association of People with Disabilities
Paid 10-week D.C. internship for students/grads with disabilities. Accessible housing and stipend included.

Emerging Leaders Internship – Viscardi Center
Paid corporate internships for students and grads with disabilities in business, STEM, or finance fields.

High School High Tech (HSHT) – The Able Trust (Florida)
Career exploration for Florida high school students with disabilities, with mentoring and paid summer internships.

MEED Program – Miami Dade College
Employment and internship support for college students with disabilities. Continuous enrollment.

EmployABILITY 305 – Miami-Dade County
Paid county internships for adults with disabilities, with job skills training and inclusion support.

Employment Programs

Workforce Recruitment Program (WRP) – U.S. Dept. of Labor
Connects college students and grads with disabilities to federal jobs and internships.

Microsoft Neurodiversity Hiring Program
Alternative hiring pathway for neurodivergent candidates (autism, ADHD, dyslexia). Includes job coaching and inclusive interviews.

Arc Broward – Arc Works
Employment readiness and placement program for adults with disabilities in Broward County. Offers training and job coaching.

Arc Palm Beach Employment Services
Job matching and supported employment for adults with developmental disabilities in Palm Beach County.

Postsecondary & Vocational Programs

Project SEARCH
Year-long internship transition program for young adults with intellectual disabilities (ages 18–22). Provides job coaching and workplace skills.

Seahawk NEST Academy – Broward College
Three-year inclusive college program for adults with intellectual disabilities. Tuition covered via FPCTP.

Marino Campus – Dan Marino Foundation
10-month vocational training for young adults with autism or developmental disabilities. Offers internships and job placement.
