Miami-Dade County Resources

Therapy Providers

Carrie Brazer Center for Autism & Alternative Approaches

The Carrie Brazer Center provides day school, early intervention, and behavioral therapy using evidence-based methods for children and young adults with autism.

Crystal Academy – Therapy Center and School

Crystal Academy in Coral Gables offers individualized interventions for children and adolescents with autism spectrum disorder and developmental delays.

Hospitals & Clinics

Nicklaus Children’s Hospital – Autism & Neurodevelopmental Program

Nicklaus Children’s Hospital provides diagnostic and therapeutic services for children with autism, ADHD, and other developmental disorders.

University of Miami – Autism Spectrum Assessment Clinic (ASAC)

The ASAC at the University of Miami offers comprehensive diagnostic evaluations and therapy services for individuals across the autism spectrum.

Schools & Programs

The Victory Center for Autism & Related Disabilities

The Victory Center provides year-round programs designed to foster independence, life skills, and inclusion for children and adults with autism.

South Florida Autism Charter School (SFACS)

SFACS is a tuition-free public charter school offering specialized instruction for students with autism in Miami-Dade and surrounding areas.

Recreation & Support

Miami-Dade Parks – Therapeutic Recreation & Inclusion (TRI)

The TRI program fosters inclusion and provides recreation and wellness opportunities for individuals with disabilities throughout Miami-Dade.

UM-NSU CARD (Center for Autism & Related Disabilities)

CARD provides lifelong, community-based support, training, and advocacy for individuals with autism and their families.
